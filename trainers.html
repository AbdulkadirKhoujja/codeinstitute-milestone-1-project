Meet Our Trainers

Get to know the supportive team behind CommunityFit Gym and find the right trainer to help you feel confident, motivated, and stronger.

Friendly Experts Here to Help

Our trainers work with members of all fitness levels. Whether you are starting out, returning to exercise, or working toward a specific goal, they are here to guide and encourage you.

Sarah Malik

Mobility and Beginner Fitness Coach

Sarah specialises in helping new members feel comfortable in the gym. She focuses on movement quality, mobility, and building confidence through realistic and supportive training plans.

Special interests: Beginner support, flexibility, mobility, confidence building

James Carter

Strength and Conditioning Coach

James helps members improve strength, technique, and consistency. His sessions focus on safe lifting, clear progress tracking, and practical routines that fit around busy schedules.

Special interests: Strength training, technique, goal setting, form improvement

Maya Thompson

Group Fitness and Cardio Coach

Maya brings energy and encouragement to every session. She leads fast-paced classes that help members improve cardio fitness while still feeling welcomed and supported.

Special interests: Group classes, motivation, cardio fitness, positive routines

Our Approach to Training

We believe fitness should be supportive, understandable, and enjoyable. Our trainers focus on helping members make steady progress without feeling overwhelmed.

Supportive Coaching

Every member is treated with patience and encouragement, regardless of their starting point or previous experience.

Clear Guidance

Trainers explain exercises in a simple way so members understand what they are doing and why it matters.

Realistic Progress

We focus on building long-term habits and sustainable improvement, not quick fixes or intimidating routines.

Personal Training Support

Members who want more focused help can speak to our trainers about personal guidance, training advice, and fitness planning.

One-to-One Guidance

Get extra help with technique, gym confidence, and routines tailored to your goals and experience level.

Goal-Focused Planning

Whether your aim is strength, general fitness, flexibility, or consistency, our trainers can help you stay on track.

Accountability and Encouragement

Working with a trainer can help you stay motivated and make progress in a clear and manageable way.
