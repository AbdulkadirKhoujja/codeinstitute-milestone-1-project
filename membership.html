Membership FAQs

Answers to common questions about joining CommunityFit Gym.

Do I need experience to join?

No experience is required. Many members join as beginners and receive guidance from trainers and staff.

Can I attend classes with any membership?

Classes are included with Standard and Premium memberships, while Basic members can upgrade or pay per class.

Is there a contract?

Memberships are flexible and designed to support members without long-term commitments.
