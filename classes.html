Our Classes

Find the Right Class for You

Our class timetable includes energising sessions, strength work, flexibility training, and beginner-friendly options that help members stay motivated.

Community Circuit

A full-body class that combines cardio, bodyweight movements, and strength stations for a fun team atmosphere.

Best for: General fitness and motivation

Stretch & Flow

A calming class focused on flexibility, movement, balance, and reducing stress while improving body awareness.

Best for: Flexibility and recovery

Spin Energy

A fast-paced cycling session that helps improve endurance, boost cardio fitness, and increase energy.

Best for: Cardio and stamina

Strength Basics

Learn core strength movements with clear coaching, safe technique, and confidence-building support.

Best for: Beginners and strength building

HIIT Blast

A short, intense workout using timed intervals to improve fitness, burn energy, and challenge yourself.

Best for: Quick, high-energy training

Beginner Fit

A welcoming introduction to fitness for members who are new to the gym or returning after a break.

Best for: Confidence and getting started

Why Join a Gym Class?

Group classes make training more enjoyable, structured, and easier to stick with over time.

Stay Motivated

Training with others helps keep you focused, encouraged, and more likely to stay consistent.

Build Confidence

Guided sessions make it easier to learn movements correctly and feel more comfortable in the gym environment.

Enjoy Variety

Mixing different class types supports your fitness goals and keeps your routine interesting.
